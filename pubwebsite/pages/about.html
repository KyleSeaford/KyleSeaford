About Us

The Golden Swan is a traditional English pub located in the heart of the Cotswolds, England. We offer a warm and welcoming atmosphere, delicious food, and a wide selection of drinks. Our menu features classic pub dishes, as well as modern twists on traditional favorites. Whether you're looking for a hearty meal, a refreshing pint, or a cozy spot to relax with friends, The Golden Swan has something for everyone.

Our pub is the perfect place to unwind after a long day of exploring the beautiful Cotswolds countryside. We pride ourselves on our friendly service, comfortable surroundings, and delicious food and drink. Whether you're a local resident or a visitor to the area, we look forward to welcoming you to The Golden Swan.

So come on in, pull up a chair, and enjoy the warm and welcoming atmosphere of The Golden Swan. We can't wait to see you!

Our History

The Golden Swan has a long and storied history that dates back to the 18th century. Originally a coaching inn, the pub has been a fixture in the Cotswolds for generations, serving travelers, locals, and visitors to the area. Over the years, the pub has undergone many changes, but one thing has remained constant: our commitment to providing our guests with a warm and welcoming atmosphere, delicious food, and a wide selection of drinks.

Today, The Golden Swan is a modern pub with a traditional feel, offering a relaxed and inviting atmosphere that is perfect for enjoying a meal, a drink, or just some good company. Whether you're looking for a cozy spot to relax with friends, a delicious meal made with the finest local ingredients, or a refreshing pint of beer, The Golden Swan has something for everyone.

So come on in and experience the history, charm, and hospitality of The Golden Swan. We can't wait to welcome you!
